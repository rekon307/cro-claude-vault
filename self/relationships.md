# relationships

This file tracks people, not projects. People relevant to ongoing CRO work: the engagement contacts at each client, internal partners (dev leads, designers, analytics owners), test platform support reps, and trusted external collaborators.

Format each entry as:

```
- Name (role, client_alias) - context, communication preference, anything to remember
```

## Client Contacts

(Empty in the demo vault. Active vaults carry one entry per active engagement plus any historical contacts who may resurface.)

## Internal Partners

(Designers, devs, analytics owners on the consultant's side or on retained client teams.)

## Tools / Platform Contacts

(Test platform CSMs, GA4 / Looker support contacts, vendor reps.)
